About Removing PDF Passwords

If you have a PDF that's password-protected and you know the password, PDFCrafty can permanently remove that protection so the file opens without a prompt in the future. This is useful for documents you own or have permission to access, such as your own encrypted reports, statements, or scanned files.

This tool requires the correct password to work — it does not attempt to crack, guess, or bypass passwords on files you don't have legitimate access to. All processing happens locally in your browser.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to know the password to unlock a PDF?+

Yes. PDFCrafty removes password protection from PDFs you already have legitimate access to, but you must enter the correct existing password to unlock the file.

Can this tool crack or guess a forgotten password?+

No. This tool only removes encryption when you provide the correct password — it cannot bypass or guess passwords on files you don't have access to.

Is unlocking a PDF private?+

Yes. Your PDF and password are processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to a server.

Will the unlocked PDF lose any content?+

No. Only the password protection is removed. All pages, text, and formatting remain exactly the same.
