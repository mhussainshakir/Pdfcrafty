Frequently Asked Questions

How do I split a PDF into separate pages?+

Upload your PDF, choose "Split into individual pages", and PDFCrafty will generate a separate PDF for every page, delivered to you as a ZIP file.

Can I extract specific page ranges instead of every page?+

Yes. Choose "Extract page range" and enter the pages or ranges you want, such as 1-3, 5, 8-10, to create a new PDF containing only those pages.

Is my file uploaded to a server when I split a PDF?+

No. PDFCrafty splits your PDF entirely within your browser. Your file never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy.

Is there a page limit for splitting PDFs?+

There's no artificial limit set by PDFCrafty. Very large PDFs (hundreds of pages) may take slightly longer depending on your device's performance.

What format will my split files be in?+

If you split into individual pages, you'll receive a ZIP file containing one PDF per page. If you extract a range, you'll receive a single PDF with just those pages.
