Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I reduce my PDF file size?+

Reduction depends on the content of your PDF. Files with large images typically shrink significantly, while text-only PDFs may see smaller reductions since they are already compact.

Will compressing reduce the quality of images in my PDF?+

PDFCrafty offers multiple compression levels. Higher compression reduces image quality slightly to save more space, while lower compression keeps quality closer to the original with a smaller size reduction.

Is compressing a PDF safe and private?+

Yes. All compression happens locally in your browser. Your PDF is never uploaded to a server.

Why didn't my file size change much?+

If your PDF is mostly text or already optimized, there may be little additional space to save. Image-heavy PDFs usually compress more noticeably.

Is there a file size limit?+

There's no artificial limit, but very large files depend on your device's available memory and processing power since everything runs in your browser.
