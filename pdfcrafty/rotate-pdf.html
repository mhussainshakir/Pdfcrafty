About Rotating PDFs

PDFs are sometimes scanned or saved with pages in the wrong orientation — sideways or upside down. PDFCrafty lets you preview every page as a thumbnail and rotate each one individually, or rotate the entire document at once, before saving the corrected file.

This tool works entirely in your browser. Your PDF's content and quality are preserved exactly — only the orientation changes.

Private by Design

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I rotate only specific pages?+

Yes. Each page thumbnail has its own rotate button, so you can rotate individual pages independently, or use "Rotate All" to rotate every page at once.

Does rotating reduce PDF quality?+

No. Rotation only changes the page orientation metadata and does not affect the content or quality of your PDF.

Is rotating a PDF private?+

Yes. Your PDF is rotated entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to a server.

What rotation angles are available?+

You can rotate pages 90 degrees clockwise, 90 degrees counter-clockwise, or 180 degrees, as many times as needed.
