Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Your Files Stay on Your Device

Every PDF tool on PDFCrafty runs entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript. When you upload a file to merge, split, compress, or convert, that file is processed locally on your own device. At no point is your file transmitted to, stored on, or processed by any PDFCrafty server. This is true for every tool on the site without exception.

What We Don't Collect

We do not collect, store, or have any access to the content of files you process using our tools. We do not require account creation, so we don't collect names, emails, or passwords for using the tools themselves.

Basic Analytics

Like most websites, we may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand which pages are visited and how the site performs, such as page views and approximate visitor counts. This data is aggregated and does not include the content of any files you process.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for the site to function, such as remembering your light/dark theme preference. We do not use cookies to track you across other websites.

Third-Party Libraries

Our tools use open-source JavaScript libraries (such as pdf-lib, pdf.js, and others) loaded from public content delivery networks. These libraries run in your browser and do not transmit your file data elsewhere.

Contact Form

If you use our contact form, any information you provide (such as your name and email) is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not shared with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Continued use of PDFCrafty after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions?

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
