About Removing PDF Pages

Sometimes a PDF contains a blank page, a duplicate, or content you no longer need. PDFCrafty lets you preview every page as a thumbnail, click to mark unwanted pages, and generate a new PDF with only the pages you want to keep — without affecting the rest of the document's content or quality.

Everything runs locally in your browser, so your file stays completely private throughout the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I select which pages to remove?+

After uploading your PDF, click on the thumbnail of any page you want to remove. Selected pages will be marked, and you can deselect them by clicking again.

Can I remove multiple pages at once?+

Yes. You can select as many pages as you like before clicking "Remove Selected Pages" to create your new PDF.

Is removing pages from a PDF private?+

Yes. Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to a server.

Can I remove all pages?+

No, at least one page must remain in the document. If you select every page, the tool will show a warning.
