About Organizing PDF Pages

Sometimes pages in a PDF end up in the wrong order — scanned out of sequence, or assembled from multiple sources. PDFCrafty shows every page as a draggable thumbnail, so you can visually rearrange your document exactly how you want it, and remove any pages you no longer need, all in one step.

This tool works entirely in your browser, keeping your document completely private throughout the process.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reorder pages?+

After uploading your PDF, simply drag each page thumbnail to its new position. The page order updates instantly as you drag.

Can I remove pages while organizing?+

Yes. Each page thumbnail has a delete button, so you can remove unwanted pages while reordering the rest.

Is organizing PDF pages private?+

Yes. Your PDF is reordered entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to a server.

Does reordering affect page quality?+

No. Pages are rearranged exactly as they are, with no compression or quality loss.
