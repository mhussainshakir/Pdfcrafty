About Adding Page Numbers

Page numbers make long documents like reports, theses, and contracts easier to navigate and reference. PDFCrafty lets you stamp page numbers onto every page of your PDF in the position and format you choose, without altering the original content.

This tool works entirely in your browser, so your document is never uploaded to a server.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I choose where the page numbers appear?+

Yes. You can place page numbers in the bottom center, bottom right, bottom left, top center, top right, or top left of each page.

Can I start numbering from a number other than 1?+

Yes. You can set any starting number, which is useful when combining this document with others or skipping a cover page.

Can I use a custom format, like "Page 1 of 10"?+

Yes. You can choose a simple number format or a "Page X of Y" format that includes the total page count.

Is adding page numbers private?+

Yes. Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to a server.
