About PDF to Excel Conversion

Extracting tables from a PDF into a spreadsheet usually means manually retyping data — a tedious and error-prone process. PDFCrafty analyzes the position of every piece of text on each page and reconstructs rows and columns based on alignment, turning your PDF's tabular data into an editable Excel file.

This works best on PDFs with clear table structures, like invoices, reports, and exported data. The entire conversion happens in your browser, so your file is never uploaded anywhere.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does PDFCrafty detect tables in a PDF?+

PDFCrafty analyzes the position of text on each page and groups text into rows and columns based on alignment, converting the detected structure into spreadsheet cells.

Will this work on any PDF, even without clear tables?+

It works best on PDFs with clearly structured tables or columns. For PDFs that are mostly free-flowing paragraphs, each line will be placed in its own row instead.

What file format will I get?+

You'll receive a .xlsx file that opens in Microsoft Excel, Google Sheets, and LibreOffice Calc.

Is this conversion private?+

Yes. Your PDF is processed and converted entirely in your browser. It is never uploaded to a server.
