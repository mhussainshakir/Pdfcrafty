About Extracting Text from PDFs

Sometimes you just need the raw text from a PDF — to paste into an email, search through quickly, or repurpose in another document. PDFCrafty reads through your PDF page by page and extracts all selectable text in reading order, giving you a clean plain-text version of your document.

This works entirely in your browser, so your file is never uploaded to a server, keeping even sensitive documents private.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I extract text from scanned PDFs?+

This tool extracts text that's already selectable in the PDF. Scanned (image-based) PDFs don't contain selectable text, so they would need OCR processing first.

Can I copy the text directly instead of downloading a file?+

Yes. After extraction, you can preview the text on the page and use the "Copy to Clipboard" button, or download it as a .txt file.

Is extracting text from a PDF private?+

Yes. The text is extracted entirely in your browser. Your PDF is never uploaded to a server.

Will the extracted text keep its original formatting?+

The text is extracted line by line in reading order, but formatting like bold, italics, fonts, and columns is not preserved since it's a plain text export.
